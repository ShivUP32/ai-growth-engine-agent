# PRD — Updated Safe AI Growth Engine

**Project:** AI Growth Engine Agent for VoiceCare AI
**Status:** Updated prototype specification
**Scope basis:** Updated `voicecare_safe_ai_growth_engine(2)` playbook compared against the earlier playbook and current development files.

Use this as the updated PRD direction.

---

## 1. Updated Problem Statement

VoiceCare AI needs to grow from roughly 2,900 LinkedIn followers to 10,000 in 90 days while also increasing how often it appears in AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI features. The engine must do this without buying leads, without buying followers, and without risky LinkedIn automation.

The updated product must now support not just content generation, but a measurable safe growth operating system:

- competitor ranking and share-of-voice tracking,
- LinkedIn and founder-led content drafting,
- existing blog/page audits for GEO/AEO/ASO,
- answer-engine citation testing,
- third-party authority opportunities,
- reverse follower growth forecasting,
- conversion assets and repurposing,
- healthcare trust and compliance guardrails.

The AI agent does not execute public-facing actions. It prepares research, drafts, scorecards, briefs, and recommendations for humans to review and execute manually.

---

## 2. Updated Goals

1. Help VoiceCare safely grow LinkedIn followers from ~2,900 to 10,000 in 90 days.
2. Improve visibility for priority RCM and healthcare AI-agent queries across Google and AI answer engines.
3. Create a repeatable weekly operating rhythm for market intelligence, LinkedIn content, blog optimization, citation building, and measurement.
4. Audit existing VoiceCare blogs/pages before creating large volumes of new content.
5. Create founder/leadership content loops that drive company-page growth without automation.
6. Identify third-party citation opportunities that can make VoiceCare more likely to be referenced by AI answer engines.
7. Convert visibility into qualified conversations through checklists, reports, comparison pages, webinar CTAs, and demo-intent pages.
8. Maintain strict compliance with LinkedIn restrictions and healthcare trust boundaries.

---

## 3. Updated Non-Goals

The updated product still must not include:

- LinkedIn scraping.
- Sales Navigator scraping.
- Browser automation inside LinkedIn.
- Auto-connects.
- Auto-DMs or auto-InMails.
- Auto-comments.
- Auto-likes.
- Auto-follows.
- Auto-profile visits.
- Fake accounts or synthetic personas.
- Engagement pods.
- Copy-paste comment campaigns across employees.
- PHI ingestion or patient-level data processing.
- Unsupported clinical, compliance, security, or ROI claims.
- Any guarantee that the follower goal will be achieved.

The system may generate manual outreach drafts and prospect research in future versions, but those are secondary to the core objective of follower growth and AI-engine/category visibility.

---

## 4. Updated Functional Requirements

The app must let a user:

1. Enter/edit company profile fields.
2. Enter current followers, follower goal, and days remaining.
3. Run any one of six agents independently.
4. Run all six agents in sequence as one full growth sprint.
5. Pass Market Intelligence outputs into LinkedIn and GEO agents automatically.
6. Enter existing blog/page URLs for audit.
7. Enter manually exported analytics from LinkedIn, Google Search Console, GA4, CRM, and manual AI-engine tests.
8. Generate a competitor share-of-voice scorecard.
9. Generate LinkedIn company, founder, employee advocacy, and partner amplification drafts.
10. Generate existing blog rewrite briefs and new GEO/AEO/ASO content briefs.
11. Generate an answer-engine citation testing protocol.
12. Generate reverse follower growth math and channel-level weekly targets.
13. Generate third-party citation opportunities.
14. Generate conversion asset maps and repurposing plans.
15. Copy/download individual agent output.
16. Download one combined growth-pack `.md` file.
17. See clear error and fallback messages.

## 7. Updated Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn ToS violation | AI never posts, connects, messages, comments, likes, follows, scrapes, or automates LinkedIn. |
| Generic AI content | Require founder POV, operational teardown, customer problem education, and human review. |
| Unsupported healthcare claims | Prompt guardrails block clinical, compliance, security, and ROI claims unless supplied/verified. |
| AI-engine visibility does not improve quickly | Add citation testing, third-party authority opportunities, existing blog audits, and original benchmark assets. |
| Follower goal is aggressive | Add reverse follower model, channel contribution targets, and weekly gap fixing. |
| Output becomes too large | Keep per-agent sections structured, allow independent downloads, and support combined growth-pack export. |
| Fallback model lacks web search | Show inline note requiring recency verification. |
